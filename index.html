Case Study Focus

Case: A solution of copper sulphate is electrolyzed using platinum electrodes. Write the reaction at cathode and why blue color fades.

Analysis:
Cathode pe \(Cu^{2+}\) reduce hokar \(Cu\) ban jayega. Blue color isliye fade hota hai kyunki \(Cu^{2+}\) ions khatam ho rahe hain solution se.
